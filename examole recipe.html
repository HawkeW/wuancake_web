Example Cafe Recipe-Worlds best scramble eggs

I adapt this recipefrom a book called Sydney Food by Bill Grainger.Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1stvisit to Bill's resterant in Kings Cross ,Sydney,I have been after the recipe.I have since transformed it into what I really belive are the bestscrambled eggs I have ever tasted.

This recipe is what I call a very special breakfast;just look at the ingredient to see why. It has to be belived.

Ingredient

The following ingredient make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream (2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pich of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives (chooed)

Introduction

  1. Whisk eggs,cream,and salt in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a none-stick pan over a high heat(taking care not to burn the butter>
  3. Using a wooden spatula,bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet,and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  4. Fold contents in again,leave for 20 seconds,and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  5. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.

You should only make a maxinum of two serving per frying pan.
